BMW 335ix Engine Rebuild

Overview

This project involved rebuilding an N55 engine and redesigning custom headlights. The goal was to improve performance and modernize the appearance. The interior and exterior are completely custom and completed by me.

What I Did

  • Disassembled and rebuilt engine components
  • Designed custom headlights and housing using CAD
  • 3D printed prototype parts for better fitment
  • Refinished interior color as well as added competition seats from a 2018 M4 BMW
  • Tested and refined final assembly

Skills Used

Mechanical CAD 3D Printing
