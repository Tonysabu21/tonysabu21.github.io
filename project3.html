Ancient Horse Project

Overview

The goal was to re-create this sculpture in real life by just using a picture online. Using different points of reference, I was able to sculpt this model and adjust depths based on shadows.

What I Did

  • Started off with a base model to modify
  • Made adjustments using CAD and other STL compatible software like Bambu Labs
  • 3D printed prototype product and made adjustments where necessary
  • Smoothed out and refined final product

Skills Used

Designing & Modeling CAD 3D Printing
