CBR600RR Honda Transmission & Wet Clutch Rebuild

Overview

This project involved rebuilding a CBR600RR's transmission and it's clutch basket in addition to its wet clutch. The goal was to improve performance and allow for a more aggressive tune. The ignition was also not working due to an attempted theft, so I custom modified my own ignition with a new key.

What I Did

  • Disassembled and rebuilt transmission and clutch components
  • Required multimeter probing and reviewing electrical diagrams of ECU
  • 3D printed parts to add to the look
  • Tested and refined final assembly

Skills Used

Mechanical CAD Electrical Diagnosing 3D Printing
