CBR600RR Transmission & Clutch Rebuild

Overview

This project involved rebuilding a CBR600RR transmission, clutch basket, and wet clutch system to improve performance and support aggressive tuning. The ignition system was also compromised due to attempted theft, requiring a custom-built ignition solution.

What I Did

Full teardown and rebuild of transmission components
Rebuilt clutch basket and wet clutch system
Used multimeter + ECU diagrams for diagnostics
Custom ignition system due to theft damage
3D printed custom visual components
Testing and refinement of final assembly

Skills Used

Mechanical Electrical Diagnosing CAD 3D Printing
